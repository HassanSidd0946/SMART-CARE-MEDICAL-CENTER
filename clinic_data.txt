--- FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ---

Q: Do I need to bring any documents for first visit?
A: Yes, please bring a valid ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Driving License) and previous medical records if available.

Q: Can I get medicine from outside pharmacy?
A: Yes, prescriptions are valid at any pharmacy. Our in-house pharmacy offers 5% discount.

Q: Is home sample collection available?
A: Yes, available for blood tests. Charges: ₹200 within 5 km, ₹400 for 5-10 km.

Q: Do you provide medical certificates?
A: Yes, medical fitness certificates available at ₹300. Sick leave certificates provided free with consultation.

Q: Are vaccination services available?
A: Yes, all routine vaccinations available. Covid-19 booster shots available daily 10 AM - 6 PM.
